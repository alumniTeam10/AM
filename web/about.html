About Us

The International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad (IIIT-H) is an autonomous university founded in 1998. It was set up as a not-for-profit public private partnership (N-PPP) and is the first IIIT to be set up under this model in India. A Governing Council consisting of eminent people from academia, industry and government presides over the governance of the institution.
